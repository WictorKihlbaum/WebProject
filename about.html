About

This website is the product of a final project in one of my current courses. The mainidea with this site is to offer you the tools you need to edit your desired images. This webapplication gives you the chance to either edit an image from your computer or one from your Google Drive.
When you are done editing you can either download the image to your computer locally or save it back to your Google Drive online.

I hope you enjoy this site as much I did making it.

Happy editing!

Worth noting: The photo editor can open images in all resolutions. However, by default, the photo editor works on a medium resolution image in order to speed up the performance of the editor. This means it will save a high-res image in lower quality.
