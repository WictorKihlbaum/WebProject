About

This website is the result of a final project in one of my current courses. The mainidea with this site is to offer my visitors the tools needed to edit their desired images. This webapplication gives you the chance to either upload a local image from your computer or fetch one from your Google Drive so that you can edit this image and afterwards either download it to your computer locally or save it to your Google Drive online.
